Hi, this is Chirag!


Hey there! Welcome to my home on the World Wide Web. I am Chirag Nagpal, currently in my sophomore year at the University of Pune, Army Institute of Technology and majoring in Computer Engineering. I have taken courses in Microprocessor Architecture, Digital Electronics and Operating Systems and my interests include Embedded and Cyber Physical Systems.

Since the summer of 2013, I have been visiting the Texas Instruments Centre for Embedded Product Design, at NSIT, Delhi and worked under Prof. DV Gadre. It is here that I picked up my hardware, hacking and fabrication skills. These days I can be found tinkering around with my BeagleBone Black! I plan to use this website to showcase my work on the net.

I recently completed research internship at the Supercomputer Education and Research Centre, Indian Institute of Science, Banagalore. I worked in the area of Machine Learning and Social Network Analysis under the guidance of Prof. N Balakrishnan. More about this can be found here.
